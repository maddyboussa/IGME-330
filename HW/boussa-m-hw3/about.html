Ecco2k Audio Visualizer


Welcome to the Ecco2k Audio Visualizer!

This app allows you to select from a set of preloaded tracks by artist Ecco2k and displays audio visualizations to the screen. Frequencies can be displayed to the screen in the form of circles and radiating lines, bars, and simple lines. You can also adjust the bass, trebel, and overal gain of the audio for custom listening. Put on some headphones, sit back and relax, and vibe!
